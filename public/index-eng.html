WHAT IS NFT METAHUNTERS?

MetaHunters is a collection of 4999 unique NFTs based on the increasingly popular TON blockchain.

NFT from the MetaHunters collection will provide access to the exciting web 3.0 game «Hunters World» and will give an advantage over other players together with the opportunity to receive bonuses from NFT staking.

An Ocultura artist has hand-painted a huge amount of external attributes to ensure that each of the 4999 NFTs is unique. But the differences will not only be visual, internal characteristics will differ as well, including:

— Rarity — from simple to LEGENDARY characters!
— Faction— 4 different factions we will tell you about soon!
— Weapons and magic — which will help you defeat other players in the game!
— Skills and bonuses — will give you an advantage over other characters!

HOW CAN I BUY NFT METAHUNTERS?

The sale of our NFT Metahunters will take place in several stages:

WHITELIST — Closed sale only for the earliest and most active members of the community. Lowest NFT Price!
PRESALE1 — First sale is for community members (price is higher than for WHITELIST)
PRESALE2 — The second sale is for community members (the price is higher than in PRESALE1).
PUBLIC SALE — Public sale is for everyone (the price is higher than in PRESALE2).

WHAT CAN I DO WITH NFT METAHUNTERS AFTER PURCHASE?

Shortly after the completion of the NFT mint, we will launch the STAKING NFT to receive bonuses. The more NFTs, the more rewards will be given out in staking!

But the most interesting thing will start after the launch of the web 3.0 game «Hunter’s World» — in the game you will get a character that will be completely identical to your NFT. You will develop faster and earn more tokens than regular players.

IS NFT METAHUNTERS A GOOD INVESTMENT?

Here are four reasons why you should buy NFTs from our collection:

PRE-SALE. When buying NFT at PRE-SALE 1 and 2, you get it at the lowest price, and those who buy NFT at PUBLIC sale will buy it at a higher price. Accordingly, you will already have an advantage.

After finishing the mint, we will start STAKING. What is staking? NFT staking is a new way to generate passive income in the world of cryptocurrencies. It allows NFT holders to lock assets on DeFi platforms and earn rewards without having to sell their NFT collections. This is the same as a deposit at interest in a bank or dividends from company shares.

Increasing audience. Every day there are more and more of us who want to mint NFT from MetaHunters. We are developing the project every day. After the launch of our web 3.0 «Hunters World» game, there will be even more members, but they will be able to buy NFTs only on the secondary market, where YOU set the price! They will have no other choice but to buy NFT at a price that you set!
